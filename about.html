About Me

A Story of Passion and Preservation

Discover the soul of Rajasthan through Sharvan's lens, where each photograph encapsulates the essence of nature, culture, and a passionate commitment to conservation

Introduction:
Embark on a visual journey through the life and lens of Sharvan Patel, a visionary photographer hailing from the serene village of Doli, adjacent to the enchanting Dhawa Doli Wildlife Sanctuary in the Jodhpur district of Rajasthan.

Early Life and Roots:
Born and raised in the picturesque village of Doli, Sharvan Patel's journey began amidst the vibrant landscapes of rural Rajasthan. His formative years in the lap of nature instilled a profound connection with the environment and a deep appreciation for the cultural tapestry woven into the region.

Educational Pursuits:
Sharvan's educational journey led him to Jodhpur, where he completed his B.Sc and B.Ed. These academic pursuits laid the foundation for a holistic approach to photography, merging his love for nature and culture with a commitment to agricultural projects.

Conservation Initiatives in Dhawa Doli Wildlife Sanctuary:
A trailblazer in conservation, Sharvan Patel has spearheaded transformative initiatives within the Dhawa Doli Wildlife Sanctuary. His commitment to preserving the sanctuary's biodiversity showcases a photographer's lens coupled with a dedicated advocate for Rajasthan's natural landscapes.

Evolution of Photography:
From capturing local moments to evolving a distinctive style, Sharvan's photography journey mirrors his personal and professional growth. His lens captures not just scenes but stories, showcasing the essence of Rajasthan with authenticity and passion.



Recognition and Achievements:
Sharvan Patel's work has garnered recognition, awards, and accolades. His photographs have been featured in exhibitions, publications, and collaborative projects, adding to the rich tapestry of his photographic accomplishments.

Future Endeavors:
Looking ahead, Sharvan Patel envisions expanding his contributions to the conservation of Rajasthan's wildlife and cultural heritage. His commitment to photography as a tool for advocacy remains unwavering, with plans to further collaborate on projects that promote environmental awareness and cultural preservation.

Join the Journey:
Explore the multifaceted narrative of Sharvan Patel's life and work. Each photograph narrates a story, and together, they unveil the beauty, authenticity, and conservation spirit that define Sharvan Patel's remarkable photographic odyssey.
